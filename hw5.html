White sugar sponge cake story

What is white sugar sponge cake

White sugar sponge cake, bái táng gāo in Mandarin or baak tong gou in Cantones, is actually hard to come by these days. This steamed cake, made from just a few ingredients has long been a favorite of young kids.

What's the magic of bai tang gao?
JUST 4 INGREDIENTS! All you need for this recipe is regular white granulated sugar, rice flour, active dry yeast, and a bit of oil for brushing (plus some water!).

Ingredients to make white sugar sponge cake

150 g rice flour (1 1/4 cups)

80 g water (plus 200g, divided; note that when measuring water, g = ml)

100 g white granulated sugar (1/2 cup)

3 g active dry yeast (about 1 teaspoon; can substitute instant yeast)

30 g lukewarm water (30 ml; about 2 tablespoons)
