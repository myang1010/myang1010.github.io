Tips About SEO

What's SEO? It stands for Search Engine Optimization. If you want more traffic to your website then take good advantage of SEO.

Good useage of SEO always starts with knowing the target audiences first. Then make a list of keywords.

Then make sure those keywords are included in below HTML elements:

  • Title, let people know what the webpage is for.
  • Meta description, which summarizes a page's content and will be shown on SERP.
  • Headings (h1, h2, etc.), keywords should be used here.
  • Hyperlinks, beside keywords, also make sure there's no broken links.
  • Content - meaningful to users
  • Accessible images and multimedia, also see if the pictures have the alt attribute with the description of the picture.
  • Responsive page, take care of the webpage and attract people to visit the website.

Especially for social media like Facebook and Twitter, the meta tags, or called Open Graph plays an important role. They help show the contents we want them to show.
