Easy Glutenous Rice Balls

Glutenous rice balls are also called tang yuan or tang tuan in mandarin. They are quick and easy to make.

The basic ingredients are sticky rice flour and water. If you want it to be a little fancy, you can add red bean paste or peanut butter.

Ingredients:

  • Sticky rice flour
  • Water
  • Red beanpaste
